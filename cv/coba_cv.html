    • Newspaper’s Business Transformation in Digital Era (Study Case) // 2016
      Presented to:
      Board of Directors

      Lead team with 8 members from 3 different departments (research, innovation, and planning) to find best strategy newspaper company use to survive in digital area by comparative study. We choose 10 newspapers from UK, United States, Australia, Brazil, and Finland.

      We used financial aspects to choose which company that successfully survive from digital disruption. The result is New York Times, Financial Times, and The Economist. They survive because they switched their revenue stream from advertising to subscription, alongside with the financial efficiency. By this research, Harian Kompas confirmed to use newspaper and digital subscription as company’s main revenue stream, not advertisement space as they used to. This decision resulted Kompas.id website launch on February 2017.
